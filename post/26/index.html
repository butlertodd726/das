Dragon Ball FighterZ is coming to PlayStation 5 and Xbox Series consoles

August 8, 2022By Ryan Galloway

Gamers on PlayStation 5 and Xbox Series consoles will soon have their chance to leverage these devices and play Dragon Ball FigherZ more than four years after its release. Today Bandai Namco announced that the game is on the way for these consoles and it will land alongside the highly anticipated and praised rollback netcode that was first announced during EVO 2022. The news was shared in a social media post that also revealed testing has shown that this DBFZ rollback is ready to go for PlayStation 5, Xbox Series, and PC and that the Bandai Namco team is working on its production.
